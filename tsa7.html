Top 6 Elden Ring Bosses This Month

Starscourge Radahn

Boss

Radahn is a demigod and one of the most formidable opponents you will face in the Lands Between. Known for his immense strength and gravity magic, Radahn is a force to be reckoned with.

Malenia, Blade of Miquella

Boss

Malenia is a warrior of unmatched skill and one of the hardest bosses in Elden Ring. Her relentless attacks and ability to heal herself make her a true test of your abilities.

Godrick the Grafted

Boss

Godrick is the ruler of Stormveil Castle and a formidable foe. Known for his grotesque appearance and grafted limbs, he will challenge your combat skills to the fullest.

Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon

Boss

Rennala is a powerful sorceress and a key figure in the game's lore. Her magic attacks and summoning abilities make her a challenging opponent in the Lands Between.

Morgott, the Omen King

Boss

Morgott, also known as Margit the Fell Omen, is a significant adversary. His agility and powerful attacks will put your skills to the ultimate test.
